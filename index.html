Currency Exchange Challenge

Smallest Unreachable

Instructions

Enter a list of bill denominations (comma-separated) or click the "Randomly Generate Denominations" button to generate a set of denominations.

Then, click "Find Smallest Unreachable" to calculate the smallest amount that cannot be made using at most one bill of each denomination.

Minimum Bills

Instructions

Enter a list of bill denominations (comma-separated) or click "Randomly Generate Denominations" to generate a set of denominations.

Next, either enter a price and payment value manually or click "Randomly Generate Price & Payment" to generate them.

Finally, click "Calculate Minimum Bills" to determine the fewest number of bills needed to provide the correct change.
